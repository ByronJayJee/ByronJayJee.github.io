Understanding the AI Energy Crisis

Byron Gaskin, PhD

Summary

This presentation examines the real-world environmental implications of artificial intelligence, focusing on energy consumption and carbon emissions from AI systems. We'll explore how the November 2022 release of ChatGPT transformed AI into a consumer product, dramatically shifting usage patterns and environmental impact. Through data analysis of major AI models, we'll compare their carbon footprints to everyday activities like car ownership and video streaming. The talk includes practical recommendations for individuals to minimize their AI-related environmental impact while encouraging transparency from AI providers around energy usage metrics.
